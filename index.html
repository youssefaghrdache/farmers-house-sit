Who we are

This isn't a restaurant we built. It's a table we already had.

My name is Youssef. This is my family's farm, a short drive and a short hike outside Essaouira, tucked above a small waterfall where the sound of the water never really stops. Long before anyone came here for lunch, this is where we ate ourselves — whatever my mother had cooked that morning, whatever my father had pulled from the ground.

Now we set a few more places at the table. When you write to us, I'll walk you in myself — down past the fields, along the stream, to the falls — and by the time we sit down to eat, you'll understand why the food tastes the way it does here. There's no menu card, no rush, and usually no one else around but us.

Come hungry. Leave your schedule at the road.
